# Ordination Platform Roadmap

Internal planning document — not public-facing.

## Vision

Hope and Truth Ministry becomes a recognized ordaining institution, similar in model to
Universal Life Church (ulc.org) and Open Ministry (open-ministry.org), with additional
value through our existing sermon archive, worship resources, contemplative content, and
stewardship consulting.

## Why this is viable

- ULC model: California 501(c)(3) nonprofit, free ordination, upsells on credentials,
  ministerial kits, certificates, and training materials. High volume, low cost per unit.
- Open Ministry: Similar model, smaller, more progressive/interfaith positioning — closer
  to H&T's identity.
- H&T differentiator: we already have the content (400+ sermons, worship resources,
  RCL calendar tools) that ordained ministers actually need. Ordination becomes a gateway
  to an ongoing pastoral support subscription.
