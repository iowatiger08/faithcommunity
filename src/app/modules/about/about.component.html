Mission & Vision

 The Faith Community Church mission is to provide a truly welcoming, open and affirming ministry to all people seeking divine wisdom, no matter where one is on lifeâ€™s journey We are committed to theology that affirms the gospel teachings and wisdom that affirms differences (rather than rejecting them) in how people come to our higher power and how people believe in the Creator and sustainer of all life. This church community values equity, access, fairness and positive ideals - leaving judgment to that Creator. Thus, we serve the community in capacities that enrich lives and advocate for causes in the way the Spirit teaches us in the Gospels and the many great wisdoms around the world who share in the universal justice and truth. We believe our purpose is to:
  • grow in love of the Holy Presence and of all our Neighbors,
  • provide a safe, sacred and faith-respective atmosphere though abundant welcoming and love of that Creator revealed in us,
  • promote, through advocacy and education positive and social awareness of issues and collective vision to the congregation and outreach to the community,
  • be an advocate for equal civil rights, diversity, enabled society, and social justice,
  • advocate for commonality and interfaith relations with non-traditional Christian teachings,
  • participate and develop coalitions with other organizations that foster social justice, the Gospels, and good citizenry.
