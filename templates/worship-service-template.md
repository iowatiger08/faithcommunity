## Notes for Worship Leaders

**Bulletin format:** This order works well as a single folded sheet (4 panels). Place the order of service on the inside; cover and back for art, welcome statement, or community announcements.

**Responsive elements:** Bolded **All:** lines should be printed clearly in the bulletin so the congregation can follow without instruction. Consider bolding or indenting congregation parts consistently.

**Scripture:** Always cite the translation. NRSV is widely familiar; CEB is more accessible for newer readers. Include chapter and verse so people can find it later.

**Communion:** This service uses an open table — all are welcome regardless of denomination or background. If your context requires a more specific invitation, adapt accordingly.

**Timing (approximate):**
- Gathering and First Sitting: 5 min
- Call to Praise through Invocation: 10 min
- Scripture and Reflection: 20–25 min
- Meditation and Pastoral Prayer: 10 min
- Lord's Prayer and Communion: 10 min
- Sending and Benediction: 5 min
- **Total: approximately 60–65 minutes**
